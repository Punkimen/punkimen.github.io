ABOUT

The year is 2050 on earth. Due to the continuous development of technology, mankind for the first time managed to create a rocket that can fly farther than

onkey
the moon and open up new horizons of space. In the history of mankind there have been multiple launches of rockets with animals and at such a moment people decided to keep up with this tradition, deciding to take an animal with them.
We all know that monkeys are the closest to humans in physiology, and this is no accident, because at that time AI rose to a new level. Multiple tests of neurochips on animals gave their results and people were able to teach monkeys to make clear decisions and understand humans. Thus, for the first time, the animal Dreamless Monkey appears, which will fly into space as a member of the crew and will perform the tasks assigned to it. The rocket flight was successful and the astronauts, together with the monkey, began to open new horizons of space.
At some point, out of nowhere, a portal appears and begins to attract a rocket. The astronauts are trying to fly away, but in the end they were dragged there. Once in a certain portal, they are thrown into another universe and by some miracle they remain alive. Having studied the area, they saw a new planet nearby and decided to land there.
This planet was somewhat similar to the earth due to the lack of gravity and the presence of oxygen. During landing on a new planet, the astronauts lost control and were unable to land safely, unfortunately they died. One monkey remained alive, initially unconscious from the landing, but soon he woke up. As a result, he realized that he was left alone on the planet where he had to survive and somehow return home. Initially, this monkey lived like an animal before being taken away for neurochip testing and at the moment he can think like a person and make decisions.
Dreamless Monkey has a dream, one day he will return home, and for this he should survive on this planet and collect a fragment of other rockets and repair his own. Most importantly, in order to launch his rocket, he needs to collect a certain Crystal containing huge energy. On its way, Dreamless Monkey will have to fight with different entities, monsters, etc.
