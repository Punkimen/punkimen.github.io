hunger games

Hunger Games is one of the high points of the adventure for the user.

It's simple - winners get everything and losers get nothing.

Эти состязания, которые становятся настоящим зрелищем – со своим сценарием, элементами шоу и драматургией, за которую будет отвечать режиссер.

These contests take place four times a year. To get access to them, for three months, which is determined by the season, you have to pump your abilities as much as possible, as well as accumulate resources.

Thus, having reached a certain progress, you can pass to the semifinals.

In the semi-finals you are waiting for special tasks, the success of which will determine your place in the final.

Materials

As in the real world, you need resources to create or develop something. ONMI has a total of 1,000,000,000 materials, which are unevenly distributed and mined.

A simple rule of thumb: The more materials mined from the ground, the lower the chance of finding new ones!

NFTs

NFTs are the primary means of creating and trading in-game items that can be used to improve player characters, collect or sell to other players.

To create NFTs, players must complete a quest, collect the necessary materials and ONM tokens, and then mine the resulting NFTs on the blockchain.

Tokenomics

ONMI has developed a scarcity-based economy where all assets, such as tokens, resources and NFTs, have value and a limited supply.

Creating in-game clothing is a necessary step for players to level up their characters, and if no one creates a certain item of clothing, it simply will not exist in the game.

Thus, players must intelligently strategize their use of resources to improve their characters and increase their chances of obtaining valuable materials, tokens, and models.

The number of tokens is tied to the number of people on the planet.

We took this number from 4 open sources and revise the number of token once a year. The idea of the number is embedded in the concept of humanity, where everyone can be part of the open world of onmi

Security

Conception

The main feature of onmi structural security is the separation of modules in the architecture of the technical implementation of the system.

Each of the active elements within the overall interaction structure performs strictly its own function, while each module is a separate program with its own source code and its own personal security concept, which indirectly affects the overall security picture as a whole.

This device is fully consistent with the philosophy of blockchain platforms.

Software sustainability

All computing processes integrated into the base of game mechanics are carried out in external environments, which allows not to overload the data storage system inside the blockchain platform.

Additional computing power within the blockchain platform is not used, because each transaction relays to the block only the necessary information on the current state of the game in order to achieve maximum transparency of mathematical processes.

2022

Pre-production and closed development.

Throughout the year, we systematically developed the core components of ONMI.

Q3 | 2023

While the team of developers and game designers created an immersive virtual reality world, key business goals were lined up.

We developed customer relationships and built relationships with strategic partners.

We also created an effective go-to-market strategy and worked on the architecture of the product.

Q2 | 2023

Developers completed a game concept and Whitepaper outlining the details of the project.

The team built a working economics of the game and built a human resources strategy. The technical infrastructure was based on microservices architecture and smart contracts. They attracted investments using a strategy of lunches and privatizations.

As a result, the project began preparing to release a testnet to present its work.

Q3 | 2023

The ONMI app was successfully launched on the App Store and Google Play Store, making it widely available to users. In addition, a convenient wallet was integrated into the app, allowing users to store and manage their assets and tokens.

The team also developed and implemented innovative AI-ML models, enhancing the unique user experience provided by ONMI. A well-defined strategy was developed to attract Web2 users to the platform, increasing the user base and engagement.

Smart contracts were thoroughly audited to ensure user reliability and security, which helped build trust and protect assets. ONMI has been successfully floated on decentralized (DEX) and centralized (CEX) exchanges, providing liquidity and accessibility to token trading.

A mainnet was enthusiastically released, allowing users to experience the full potential and functionality of ONMI.

Weather: Real-time weather effects on the in-game map that affect rewards.

Totems: Unique locations with totems that restore energy and eliminate wear and tear on the ONMI.

PvP Zones: Zones on the map designed for PvP battles

OMI Showcase: Users can place their OMI in various places on the map, showing them off to other users and collecting likes.
