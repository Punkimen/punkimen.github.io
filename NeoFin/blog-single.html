Why You Need No-Code Solution to Launch Your Lending Startup

In the wake of the lockdown, the need for a boost in the digital capacities of financial institutions became more pressing. There was a drastic and sudden change in consumer behavior as well as a decline in an employee capacity.

Innovation compliance does not come easily for banks due to the demands of the legal system or financial engagements. That is why fintech startups are hastily exploring the potential of the lending market, and no-code solutions come to the rescue to help them achieve the results faster.

The time needed to integrate a standard lending software into the core system is much, as it may take 6-9 months. It also requires the involvement of IT specialists that leads to staffing, educating, and retaining a whole in-house team or paying an outsourcing one.

A survey of 2,025 IT professionals in 2021 across the United States, the United Kingdom, China, Belgium, Germany, and the Netherlands revealed that 57% agreed that the demand for IT professionals is increasing. About 76% believed that the need for IT professionals is reaching an all-time high, while about 61% attested that the cost of software projects has escalated. As a result, the implementation of no-code solutions for startups is a step on the right track.

All you need to benefit from no-code solutions is to select software with all the functionality that addresses your consumer's needs. You do not have to write a single line of code for your app to be up and running, so the time gained in this process will be invested in other business areas.

By adopting no-code solutions, capital investments are reduced to a minimum since you might not need any IT specialists as all the needs have been cared for by your no-code solution provider. Consequently, you will not need to staff an in-house IT department to integrate and customize lending software. Also, you can avoid any maintenance costs while running your day-to-day business.

One of the disadvantages of running your lending startup with a complex system is the length of time spent on programming and launching. Innovative no-code lending solutions, such as Neofin, give you a reduced development time, and within a day, your lending application can be available online.
